What is a short story?

The simplest answer is that it is exactly that: short. It should have an impact and a meaning, and it should be memorable.

How long should my short story be?

  1. The short-short story is around 1 000 to 1 500 words

  2. The short story is 2 000 to 5 000 words or longer

  3. The novella is 30 000 to 60 000 words

  4. The average novel is 80 000 words

Anything under 1000 words is considered flash fiction.

These are guidelines, and the length is determined by the story itself. A better description of a short story is an account of an incident. It usually contains:

  1. a single main character (protagonist)

  2. a simple plot structure

  3. a clear beginning, middle, climax and end

There is usually no sub-plot, and some of the secondary characters are one-dimensional. However, we create a multi-dimensional main character who undergoes a change in the course of the story.

The main character in the beginning of the story should not be entirely the same person at the end. Something happens. Something changes. He or she gains an insight or changes direction. The person grows or weakens because of a dramatic event.

The goal with the modern short story is to keep it fast-paced, with original striking descriptions or imagery, sparsely and cleverly applied.
