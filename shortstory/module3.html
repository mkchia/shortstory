A short story is a different thing all together - a short story is like a kiss in the dark from a stranger. ~Stephen King

We write what we read, so if we want to write short stories we have to read short stories. You must learn to read like a writer. Choose a short story, read it and answer the answer the questions below.

Exercise 5 - Read another short story.

  1. In which ways does it differ from the first?

  2. Did you like it more, or less?

  3. Why?

Repeat this exercise for every short story you read in future. The more you read, the more you will learn.
