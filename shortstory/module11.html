Exercise 18 - Sequel

Now write the sequel to the scene you’ve written.

You need to help your character deal with the emotional turmoil you created in the beginning. Allow the decision that the main character makes here to be his/her overall story goal.

Follow the steps of Sequel closely:

  1. Emotion

  2. Quandary

  3. Decision

  4. (the beginning of the next) action

End it here. The moment the character begins acting again, you are in the next scene.
